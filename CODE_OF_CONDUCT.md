
# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and inclusive
- Accepting constructive criticism
- Showing empathy towards others

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Offensive comments
- Personal attacks

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Project maintainers will follow these guidelines in determining consequences:

- Warning
- Temporary ban
- Permanent ban
